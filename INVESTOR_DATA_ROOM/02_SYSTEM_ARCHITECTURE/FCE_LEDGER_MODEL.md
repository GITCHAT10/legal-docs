# 💰 FCE LEDGER MODEL (MIRA-ALIGNED)

## Financial Principles
- **Deterministic Totals**: All tax calculations are computed per line-item using Half-Up 2-decimal rounding.
- **Append-Only**: Financial truth is maintained as a continuous stream of events. No 'In-Place' updates.
- **Fail-Closed Pre-auth**: All charges are validated against credit limits before being sealed in the ledger.

## Maldives Tax Engine
- **Service Charge**: 10%
- **TGST**: 17% (Applied to Base + Service Charge)
- **Green Tax**: $6/pax/night (Strict enforcement of 12-hour stay and child exemption rules).

## Reconciliation Policy
The system performs continuous 3-way reconciliation (PMS vs FCE vs MIRA Export) to ensure zero unresolved variance.
