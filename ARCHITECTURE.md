# 🔗 INTEGRATION SCOPE — ENTIRE ECOSYSTEM WORKFLOW

This interface must connect across the full operating chain:

## 1. BUBBLE — Demand Entry Layer
BUBBLE captures demand (orders, bookings, requests) and sends it to ELEONE.

## 2. ELEONE — Core Decision Engine
The brain that decides fulfillment, pricing, logistics, and compliance rules.

## 3. ELEONE INN — Hospitality Operations + MIRA Logic
Captures hospitality data (reservations, billing, tax) and feeds it back to ELEONE.

## 4. SKYGODOWN — Supply Consolidation Layer
Manages supplier consolidation, storage, and shipment preparation.

## 5. ATOLLAIRWAYS — Air Capacity Layer
Orchestrates air movement capacity (charter, ACMI, cargo).

## 6. AEGIS / ACI — Control, Audit, and Enforcement Layer
Monitors all layers for anomalies and provides real-time control and enforcement.

---

# 🔄 FULL OPERATING LOOP
**BUBBLE captures demand, ELEONE decides, ELEONE INN feeds hospitality data, SKYGODOWN consolidates supply, ATOLLAIRWAYS moves urgent air traffic, and AEGIS monitors and controls the entire loop.**
