# MALDIVES DIGITAL ECONOMY ACT (LEGAL DRAFT)

## ARTICLE 1 — PURPOSE
To establish a national digital economic infrastructure system for real-time recording of economic transactions.

## ARTICLE 2 — NATIONAL TRANSACTION SYSTEM
All commercial transactions shall be recorded through a certified digital economic platform (iMOXON).

## ARTICLE 3 — TAX COMPUTATION
Taxes shall be computed automatically based on standardized digital rules:
- Base Price
- Service Charge (10%)
- GST/TGST equivalents (17%)

## ARTICLE 4 — DATA GOVERNANCE
Economic data is classified as national economic infrastructure data and subject to sovereign protection laws.

## ARTICLE 5 — SYSTEM INTEGRATION
Private sector systems (POS, fintech, marketplaces) must integrate via approved iMOXON APIs.

## ARTICLE 6 — EMERGENCY ECONOMIC CONTROLS
Government may activate price stabilization, supply prioritization, and emergency distribution networks.

## ARTICLE 7 — AUDITABILITY
All transactions must be traceable, immutable, and auditable in real time via SHADOW.
