# iGEO Sovereign Platform - Executive Summary
## Maldives International Group (MIG) | UEI: 2024PV12395H

### Vision
To provide the Republic of Maldives with a sovereign, automated infrastructure and design engine that replaces fragmented consultancy with a unified, deterministic AI operating system. iGEO (integrated Global Engineering Operations) is built on the **MNOS (Maldives NextGen Operating System)** architecture.

### Core Technology Layers (The ASI Engine)

1.  **Sovereign Intelligence (Eleone & Shadow):**
    *   **Eleone Decision Layer:** Every engineering decision is hashed and authorized via SHA-256 logic, ensuring non-repudiation.
    *   **Shadow Ledger:** An immutable audit trail that tracks every design iteration, fiscal calculation, and compliance check.

2.  **Engineering & Infrastructure (iGEO Flow):**
    *   **Sewer & Drainage:** Real-time hydraulic modeling using Manning's Equation, optimized for Maldives' specific topography and rainfall data.
    *   **AquaSync™ (Water OS):** A high-fidelity Reverse Osmosis (RO) simulation engine replicating DuPont WAVE PRO performance with patented feed-dilution logic for energy-efficient desalination.
    *   **HACEOSLAR (Energy OS):** A "Renewable-First" power management algorithm (RFL) that synchronizes solar, battery storage, and diesel backup for zero-interruption island grids.

3.  **Spatial & Layout (SIE Engine):**
    *   **Deterministic Geometry:** Enforces a mandatory 12ft usable depth guard and single-loaded corridor logic for narrow plot developments.
    *   **AEGIS Compliance:** Automated verification against Maldives FAR (Floor Area Ratio) limits, ventilation requirements, and fire safety codes.

4.  **Fiscal & Procurement (FCE / MOATS):**
    *   **MOATS Fiscal Engine:** Hard-coded MIRA compliance, calculating 10% Service Charge, 17% TGST, and Green Tax ($6/pax) with 100% accuracy.
    *   **Automated BOQ:** Generates structural steel tonnage, concrete volume, and mobilization costs directly from geometric outputs.
