# 🌊 BLACK CORAL FUTURES™: CSR Impact Pitch

## Vision
"From Zero Opportunity to Global Hospitality Careers." We provide a verified pathway for underprivileged Maldivian youth into luxury service roles.

## The Solution
- **Free Foundation**: Zero-cost entry-level training.
- **UHA Verification**: Skills audited via the Black Coral Standard on the SHADOW Ledger.
- **Resort Deployment**: Hands-on training at partner resorts (SALA/Prestige).
- **Employment Pathway**: Priority hiring and global mobility support.

## Impact Flywheel
1. **Donor Funding**: Trains a cohort of underprivileged youth.
2. **UHA Verification**: Ensures graduates are job-ready and verified.
3. **Resort Deployment**: Improves service quality and drives revenue.
4. **Reinvestment**: A portion of revenue funds the next cohort.

## Investment Ask ($100,000 Pilot)
- **12 Students**: 🌱 Seed Tier ($25,000)
- **25 Students + Infrastructure**: 🌊 Catalyst Tier ($50,000)
- **Full Pilot + Roadmap**: 🏆 Transform Tier ($100,000)
