# MNOS Frontend Authority Boundaries

Enforcement Policy:
- No identity authority.
- No payment authority.
- No tax authority.
- No settlement authority.
- No audit authority.
- No booking truth.
- No procurement truth.
- No education record truth.
- No legal truth.
- No medical truth.
- No logistics truth.
- No AI execution authority.

Every frontend must call MNOS APIs only. The interface is a view of the truth, not the truth itself.
