# UT AEROMARINE Doctrine

## Core Principles
1. **Hybrid Sovereignty**: Air and Sea unmanned operations must be governed by a single, unified control layer (MNOS).
2. **Fail-Closed by Design**: Any breach of safety, authority, or compliance results in an immediate mission halt or Return-to-Base (RTB).
3. **Forensic Accountability**: Every mission stage, from intent to recovery, must be hashed and sealed in the SHADOW ledger.

## Operating Rules
- NO PERMIT → NO MISSION
- NO AEGIS OPERATOR → NO LAUNCH
- NO REGISTERED DEVICE → NO ARMING
- NO AIRSPACE CLEARANCE → NO AUTONOMOUS ROUTE
- NO DYNAMIC HOME POINT FOR BOAT LAUNCH → NO TAKEOFF
- NO GEOFENCE → NO ROUTE APPROVAL
- NO SHADOW TRACE → NO MISSION START
- NO SHADOW SEAL → NO FCE BILLING
- NO EVENT → NO INVOICE
