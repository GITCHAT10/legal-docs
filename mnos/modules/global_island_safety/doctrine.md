# MIG SHIELD Global Island Safety OS Doctrine

## Core Principles
1. **Local Emergency, National Accountability**: Incidents are managed locally by resorts or councils but remain visible to national authorities for compliance and support.
2. **Authorized Response Only**: Drones respond only after AEGIS identity verification and ORCA risk approval.
3. **Restricted Observation**: CCTV observes only under role-based rules. Surveillance is for public safety, not unrestricted monitoring.
4. **Forensic Proof**: SHADOW proves every action, every decision, and every mission outcome.

## Operating Rules
- **No Permit → No Mission**: Aviation and security permits are mandatory.
- **No Authority → No Access**: Evidence access is strictly case-based.
- **MNDF/Police Separation**: National security (MNDF) is separated from public safety/traffic (Police) workflows.
- **Fail-Closed Compliance**: Country profiles adapt compliance workflows but cannot weaken core safety rules.

## Global Privacy Guardrails
- No unrestricted surveillance.
- No public facial recognition by default.
- No evidence export without incident or case ID.
- SHADOW logging is required for all public CCTV access.
