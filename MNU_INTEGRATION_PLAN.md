# 🇲🇻 MNU × UHA National Integration Plan

## Vision
To build a sovereign talent infrastructure for the Maldives by aligning MNU academic excellence with UHA Black Coral Standard verification.

## 4-Layer Model
1. **Academic Credit Mapping**: MNU courses map to Black Coral pillars (HACCP/ISO/UNESCO/MICHELIN) with a 30% weighting.
2. **National Verification Network**: EDGE nodes deployed atoll-wide (Addu, Malé, Fuvahmulah) for practical assessment.
3. **Sovereign Credential (UCO-National)**: Issuing MNU-linked credentials recognized by the Ministry of Tourism.
4. **National Talent Dashboard**: Real-time pipeline visibility for the Ministry and Resort partners.

## Pilot Phase (Q1-Q2 2026)
- **Scope**: 20 students from MNU Diploma in Hospitality.
- **Locations**: Addu & Malé.
- **Goal**: Issue the first 10 UCO-National credentials by June 2026.
