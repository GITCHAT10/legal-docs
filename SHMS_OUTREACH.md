# 🎓 SHMS × MARS Academy: Partnership Proposal

## Vision
To bridge the gap between Swiss academic excellence and Maldivian real-world operational execution through audited digital-to-physical training.

## Collaboration Models
1. **Credit Mapping:** Recognition of MARS L2-L4 certifications as ECTS-equivalent practicum credits.
2. **Hybrid Internship:** SHMS students deploy to partner resorts using EDGE + SHADOW tracking.
3. **Content Exchange:** Faculty provide theory → MARS converts to AIRMOVIE SOPs + live drills.

## The SHADOW Advantage
Every student task is cryptographically verified by resort managers and logged to the SHADOW ledger, providing SHMS with:
- 100% visibility into internship performance.
- Tamper-proof competency reports.
- Real-time compliance scoring.
